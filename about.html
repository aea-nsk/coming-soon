About AD Exams Academe & Analytor

Empowering Civil Services Aspirants with Guidance, Analytics & Wellness

Who We Are

AD Exams Academe (AEA)- Formerly known as AD's IAS Academy, founded by Anil Dabhade, is an education initiative dedicated to UPSC & MPSC aspirants across India.

Analytor is our next-generation civil services preparation ecosystem combining structured study plans, analytics, AI-guided feedback, and wellness support designed specifically for Indian aspirants.

Our Mission

  • Provide reliable and scientific UPSC/MPSC preparation tools
  • Deliver personalised daily & weekly study plans
  • Empower aspirants through analytics and AI-driven reports
  • Improve mental wellness and consistency with AWS (Analytor Wellness System)

Why Aspirants Trust Us

  • Transparent pricing in INR — no hidden charges
  • AI-powered study plans, analytics & personalised progress tracking
  • Made in India, built for Indian civil services aspirants
  • Support for multiple roles: Aspirant, Mentor, Setter, Admin
