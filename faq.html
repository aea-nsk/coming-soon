About Analytor

1. What is Analytor? ›
Analytor is a scientific preparation platform for UPSC & MPSC aspirants, combining study planning, analytics, AI-driven support and wellness tools.

Analytor is created and managed by AD Exams Academe (AEA), founded by Anil Dabhade.

Aspirant Experience

6. Does Analytor include test-taking? ›
Yes. Analytor includes MCQ tests, mains uploads, explanations, and detailed performance analytics.

Yes. Analytor builds AI-guided daily, weekly and long-term plans based on your preparation style.
