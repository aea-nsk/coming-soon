About Analytor

1. What is Analytor?
Analytor is a scientific preparation platform for UPSC & MPSC aspirants, combining study planning, analytics, AI-driven assistance and wellness tools.
2. Who manages Analytor?
Analytor is created and managed by AD Exams Academe (AEA), founded by Anil Dabhade.

Subscriptions & Payments

3. What subscription plans do you offer?
We offer 1, 3, 6, 9 and 12-month plans starting from ₹555. Full details available on the Pricing page.
4. How are payments handled?
Payments are securely processed by Razorpay. We do not store your card or banking information.
5. Is there a refund policy?
Refunds are allowed only for duplicate transactions, failed activations, or accidental multiple payments. Full policy is available on the Refund page.

Aspirant Experience

6. Does Analytor include test-taking?
Yes. Analytor includes MCQ tests, mains answer uploads, explanations, and analytics to help you measure progress.
7. Does Analytor offer personalised study plans?
Yes. Analytor creates AI-guided daily, weekly, and milestone-based plans tailored to your preparation style.
8. What is AWS (Analytor Wellness System)?
AWS is our wellness engine that provides breathing timers, fatigue detection, affirmations, focus streak engine and motivation tools to support consistency.
