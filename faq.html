Clear answers to common UPSC & MPSC aspirant doubts

About Analytor

1. What is Analytor? ›
Analytor is a scientific preparation platform for UPSC & MPSC aspirants, combining study planning, analytics, AI-driven support and wellness tools.

• Intelligent Study Planner
• Prelims + Mains + Optional mapping
• Progress analytics & weak-area detection
• DARE Revision Engine
• AWS (Analytor Wellness System)
• MCQ tests & mains answer uploads

Everything works together to guide you through a structured and consistent study journey.
2. Who manages Analytor? ›
Analytor is created and managed by AD Exams Academe (AEA), founded by Anil Dabhade.
It is an independent, educational, analytics-driven learning platform.

Subscriptions & Payments

3. What subscription plans do you offer? ›
Analytor offers flexible plans: 1, 3, 6, 9, and 12 months. All plans include full access to the complete system — planner, analytics, tests, DARE, AWS, and progress tracking.

Visit the Pricing page for details.
4. How does the subscription work? Will content repeat if I re-subscribe? ›
No. Analytor uses a **Cycle-Based Learning Model**:

• Cycle 1 — Full syllabus coverage
• Cycle 2 — Advanced preparation + deeper tests
• Cycle 3 — Mastery + PYQ strengthening
• Cycle 4 — Maintenance + targeted revision

Your progress, tests, weak areas and completed topics continue from where you left off. Re-subscribing extends your time, not your content.

7. How does Analytor calculate the time required for UPSC/MPSC preparation? ›
Analytor uses a realistic model based on:

• Your selected exam (UPSC/MPSC)
• Your optional subject
• Your daily study capacity (2h, 3h, 4.5h, 6h etc.)
• Syllabus depth for Prelims + Mains + Optional (2 papers)
• Your speed profile (Fresher/Repeater/Working/Homemaker)

The planner then computes days, months and expected finish date.
8. How long does it realistically take to complete UPSC or MPSC syllabus? ›
The time varies based on your daily hours:

• At 2 hours/day → Long-term multisession cycle
• At 3–4.5 hours/day → Balanced cycle completion
• At 6+ hours/day → Faster cycle with revision loops

The Planner Time Console shows exact estimates.
9. Does Analytor give personal suggestions? ›
Yes. The system adjusts your plan based on:

• Accuracy trends
• Topic speed
• Weak spot patterns
• Revision backlog
• Optional subject depth

This is done automatically as you study.

Features & Tools

10. What is the DARE Revision Engine? ›
DARE (Daily Active Revision Engine) schedules your revisions automatically. It analyses:

• What you studied • What you forgot • Your accuracy • Your study gaps

Then it generates daily/weekly revision tasks.
11. What is AWS (Analytor Wellness System)? ›
AWS is your wellness companion. It includes:

• Breathing timers
• Focus streak tracking
• Fatigue alerts
• Motivational cues
• Affirmations & mood-support tools

Designed to help you stay consistent during long study months.
12. Does Analytor include test solving? ›
Yes. You can: • Generate MCQ tests
• Attempt topic-wise practice
• Upload mains answers
• Track strengths & weaknesses
• Analyse accuracy and speed
• Export performance history
